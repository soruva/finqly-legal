Frequently Asked Questions (FAQ)

Last updated: October 20, 2025

Is Finqly free to use?

Yes! Most features are free. Optional premium features may be offered through one-time purchases or subscriptions.

Do I need to sign up or log in?

No login is required. Finqly is designed to be fully usable without any personal account.

What kind of insights does Finqly provide?

Finqly helps you reflect on your emotions and habits related to finance and personal growth. It offers educational insights, emotion tracking, and trend reflection.

Is Finqly giving me financial advice?

No. Finqly is not a financial advisor and does not offer financial recommendations. The app is for educational and reflective purposes only.

Does Finqly use AI?

Currently, Finqly’s feedback is based on predefined logic and user inputs. AI features may be introduced in the future, but they will remain educational and not constitute professional advice.

How is my data stored?

All data stays on your device. We do not collect, store, or transmit any personal data to servers.

Can I delete or reset my records?

Yes. You can reset your saved trends and history from the app settings at any time.

Is customer support available?

Finqly is independently developed and does not offer individual support. Most common questions are answered in this FAQ or through in-app help sections.

What if the app crashes or behaves unexpectedly?

Please try restarting the app. If issues persist, ensure you are using the latest version. You may also report problems via our Google Form.

How can I access the legal documents?

You can view our Privacy Policy, Terms of Service, and Disclaimer through the app’s settings menu or at our official legal site: Finqly Legal Center.

How can I request data removal or opt out?

While Finqly does not collect personal data, you may request deletion of your local history or raise concerns via our Google Form.
